ABOUT CONNECT GLOBAL

Connect Global is a body of diverse organizations, churches, and ministries from various backgrounds and spheres of influence. Though we are many and each unique, we are led together in unity by the Lord, firmly rooted in our collective vision of limitless impact in our world. Our unity is not just in word but is a reflection of the Lord's guiding hand, leading us through each season with the same divine direction.

CONNECT CONFLUENCE

"Connect Confluence" by Connect Global is an annual gathering of men, organizations, churches, and ministries from all walks of life, infused with the divine presence of God. Hosted by Apostle Niyi Makinde, and led also by other powerful ministers alike, the confluence is a hub of transformative teachings, impartations, activations, and healings by the Spirit of God.

It is a meeting of global influencers, intellectual luminaries, top-tier leaders, revolutionary entrepreneurs, iconic figures, and political and government leaders, including presidents, senators, governors, ambassadors, ministers, and many others. We convene every first week of the year, in January, to connect and receive God's message, prophecies and instructions for the year.

At "Connect Confluence," participants bow in reverence to God, listening intently to His guidance for the year ahead. The gathering serves as a spiritual wellspring, where attendees connect meaningfully with God and with one another, sharing in the transformative power of God's word and divine theme for the year.
