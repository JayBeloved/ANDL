ABOUT NIYI MAKINDE

I'm Niyi Makinde. My mission is clear:

  • To raise ministers and leaders for God.
  • To birth ministries in sons and daughters.
  • To serve as spiritual oversight and apostolic covering for various bodies and extensions.

BASED ON THESE MISSION:

  • We will rebirth all.
  • We will connect all.

MY BELIEF:

  • Is that God has plans for everyone and can take the most unlikely and use him powerfully.
  • Is that Christians should be thoroughly steeped in kingdom values and highly relevant in the secular world.
  • Is that Christians should be able to influence the society with kingdom principles.

MY FOUNDATIONAL SPIRITUAL TEACHERS:

Evangelist Stephen Owoigbe

This gracious man was an assistant pastor to my biological dad many years ago. He's the one who taught me how to preach in church and at crusade even before I was a teenager. I can boldly say that he gave me the foundational training for ministry. This man brought the evangelistic side of me out.

Pastor Tobi Adeoye

This wonderful man introduced me to the teachings and patterns of Bro. Gbile Akanni. He introduced me to discipleship before I gained admission to university. Within the seven years of my relationship this man of God, it was a wonderful one. I sometimes share with people that God took me through leadership and discipleship before he brought me into apostleship.

Pastor S.A. Oloyede

This man gave me platforms in the church to express my gifts. This man brought the teaching and pastoring gifts out of me. He recommended me for ordination as assistant pastor at age nineteen.

MY SPIRITUAL FATHER:

Pastor E.A. Adeboye

To be sincere, I am one raised by God. God took me into the wilderness for years to train me. I have read many books by various authors and listened to many preachers. But one thing stood out for me. God clearly spoke to me the following words - I TOOK YOU THROUGH THIS PROCESS TO RAISE YOU BY MYSELF BUT I AM CONNECTING YOU TO THE GRACE AND COVENANT OF GOD UPON PASTOR E.A. ADEBOYE. YOU SHALL BE HIS SON, AND HE SHALL BE YOUR FATHER.

MORE ABOUT ME

Niyi Makinde is an apostle, author and crusader. Many know him for healings and miracles. Having written hundreds of books on multiple subjects, great leaders recommend his books. He speaks and writes with high inspiration and creative wisdom.

Apostle Niyi Makinde is:

  • The Founder and General Overseer of Rebirth Global Church (a network of churches across the globe).
  • The Founder of Connect Global (a larger body of churches and ministries across the globe).
  • The Pioneer and Father of the NEW APOSTOLIC AND PROPHETIC MOVEMENT OF NIGERIA (NAPMON)
  • The Author of Over 300 books
  • The Host of the annual Connect Confluence (a global gathering of various believers, churches and ministries).
  • The Author of NEW BEGINNINGS TODAY (A 1-year Daily Devotional for adults)
  • The Author of YORETO (A quarterly devotionals for Teenagers)

Niyi Makinde is highly esteemed and respected by great leaders. Known for humility, simplicity, integrity, great faith and uncommon wisdom. Many great leaders consider him as one of the wisest and greatest among men. He is a high authority leader, father figure and spiritual oversight over many churches, ministries and Christian networks.
