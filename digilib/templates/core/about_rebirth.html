Rebirth Global Church

Raising up ministers and leaders for the Lord

"Go, raise up ministers and leaders who will impact cities, countries, and continents to My glory."

– Almighty God

OUR DIVINE VISION

A divine vision was bestowed upon Apostle Niyi Makinde in the year 2010. The vision was to raise up ministers and leaders for the Almighty. Prior to this, he had been spreading the word of God through his preaching and teaching. However, after receiving the vision, he began to organize various other meetings, programs, and retreats.

On April 15, 2015, God revealed to him the name of the ministry - "Rebirth." Initially, it was referred to as "Rebirth Network," but in 2017, it was renamed to "Rebirth Global" after God revealed the next phase of the ministry's vision – to raise up ministers and leaders who would impact cities, countries, and continents to the glory of God.

OUR JOURNEY

Rebirth Global Church began its journey by organizing programs for campus students. The first campus church was founded by Pastor (now Reverend) Dele Emiola in February 2016 at the Abraham Adesanya Polytechnic in Ijebu-Igbo, Nigeria. Many other campus churches have been established across various locations.

Following the Spirit's leading, Apostle Niyi travelled to Port Harcourt, Nigeria in January 2017. It was there that God spoke to him, instructing him to go to Ijebu-Ode and start a church. In August of that same year, with only four members including himself, he began the church in a brother's sitting room. From its humble beginnings, Rebirth Global Church has grown and expanded under the blessings of God.

OUR CORE VALUES

In Rebirth Global Church, every member is great and global. The teachings of God are not just theoretical but are practically demonstrated through the Holy Spirit, and every individual is equipped to fulfill their divine purpose on earth. The conviction is strong that as a follower of Jesus Christ, and a member of Rebirth Global Church, you will make a difference in your world.

Our Guiding Principle

Every member is a minister and leader, and great and global. Every member is nurtured by the Lord to discover their innate qualities and unleash their full potential. This is the core of Rebirth Global Church's ethos, and it remains our guiding principle, inspiring everyone to embrace their greatness and make a difference in the world.
